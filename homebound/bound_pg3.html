Design Principles applied

What principles I will use:

  • Good lore
  • Responsive Controls
  • Optomized for smooth running
  • Modular code for trouble shooting and optomization
  • Simple and clean GUI
  • Challenges and minigames for added hook and loop
  • Small world made large through content and personality

The goal is an immersive and fun experience all while being relaxed and go at your own pace. I will be takng the slightly risky route of a purely story driven game. Which may end up bottle necking the player sometimes.
