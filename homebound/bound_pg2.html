Good design principles

This is a general guide, not all principles have to be applied.

My 3 focus points:

  • Good lore or story
  • Responsive interactions
  • Optomized

More info about Dan Harmon's story cycle [story cycle:link]

Smooth GUI:

Bad GUI is the death of a game.

Tight and Adequate Player Controls:

That allow the player to interact with the world that has been built.

Interactive System // Goals, Rules, and Challenges:

That guide the player and give the game meaning, fairness, satisfaction and engagement.

Gameplay, Story, Art Design, and Graphics:

That create a coheasive and appealing game world and mechanics.

Loop // Continuos Challenges:

That give flexibility and immediate rewards. This will keep the player hooked and motivated
